Pool & Risk Parameters

Settings Guide

  • LOW/MID/HIGH Width: narrower = tighter concentration, wider = safer inventory.
  • Sigma Threshold: higher means fewer mode switches.
  • Correlation Impact: high impact widens ranges more when correlation weakens.
  • Recenter Trigger: default 75 means re-center when ratio drifts 75% of current width.
  • Fee Setting: flat friction deducted from swap revenue each trade.
