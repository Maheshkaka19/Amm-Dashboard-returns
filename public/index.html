🔄 Walk-Forward Optimizer

Trains on last N days to find the {width, cooldown, buffer} that maximised net harvest quality, then applies those parameters for the next N days. Grid: 48 candidates × every window. Yellow dashed lines on chart = optimizer updates.

📐 ATR-Based Band (replaces fixed %)

Active width = max(base_width, ATR₁₄ × multiplier). ATR₁₄ = rolling 14-hour mean of |ratio return|. During volatile regimes the ATR floor prevents over-tight bands that trigger constant recenters. During quiet regimes, base_width dominates.

🛡️ Anti-Churn Controls

Cooldown 24h: after a recenter, block the next for 24 hours. Override if drift > width × 4.0 (emergency).
Profit Buffer 1.0×: swap only if net > 1.0× brokerage cost. Raise to 2.0× to filter reversing noise.
