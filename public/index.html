Capital

Split 50 / 50 between the two assets at open price. Rounded to whole shares.

🎯 Concentration Band

Tighter band (0.5–1%) → more swaps, lower IL per trade, more recenters needed. Wider band (3–5%) → fewer recenters, larger IL spikes, fewer swaps. Cooldown prevents back-to-back recenters in choppy markets.

Brokerage

Zerodha / NSE standard: 0.15% + 0.15% = 0.30% round-trip. A swap only executes when net profit exceeds zero after brokerage.
